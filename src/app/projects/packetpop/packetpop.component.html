PacketPop

A single or multiplayer game based on the simplest activity - Popping bubbles!

About

It's an online game of bubblewrap! Each player gets to choose a gem or color that they prefer. Then, in a single or multiplayer game, they have a not-so-simple goal!

Why does it get complicated? The game board is multi-colored. Each gem of the player's own color is limited, and it grants bonuses when you pop your selected bubbles. Then, there are potentially other players. Unless it's a private room, players can join games in progress, and at that point, it becomes a frenzy!
