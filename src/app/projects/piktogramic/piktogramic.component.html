Piktogramic

Piktogramic - The GIF Game!

Piktogramic is a multiplayer Pictionary-like game. Think about regular pictionary - except within a chat room of other players and with animated video clips!

About

Designed around the classic format of pictionary, Piktogramic brings the game to the Net. It features a theater-style format, where the clues to the word that the admin or system chooses is displayed prominently.

Each round runs until either a single or a small group of players guesses the correct word and win, or it runs out of images. If nobody guesses correctly, the round is lost.

Currently undergoing a redesign, Pitkogramic was featured on Product Hunt when it launched!
